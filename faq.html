FAQ

DO you accept walk-ins?

Sorry, not at this time. We are currently only accepting appointments only.


How should I prepare for my procedure?

Please refer to Before & Aftercare.


Do you take custom designs?

Yes! We love custom designs. Please allow us at least a minimum of 3 days to customize your image.


How much do you charge for custom designs?

Yes, custom designs has an additional fee ranging from $30 - $60 that has to be paid before drafting starts.


Do you provide numbing?

Yes, if you feel you need it, please let us know, as you will have to arrive 30 minutes early to have sufficient time for the numbing to take effect.


Do you refund the booking fee?

All reservations require a booking fee and are NON-REFUNDABLE under any and all circumstances or reasons. Please refer to POLICY.


What style of tattoo do you offer?

We offer mini, finger, and fine line, and occasional detailed designs.


Do you charge for touch-up?

Touch-up is charged based on your tattoo size and details. Starting at as low as $30.


Can I workout after my procedure?

Please refer to Before & Aftercare.
